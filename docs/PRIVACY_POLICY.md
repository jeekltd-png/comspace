## 1. Introduction

Welcome to **ComSpace** ("we," "our," "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our e-commerce platform, including our website and mobile applications (collectively, the "Platform").

By accessing or using the Platform, you agree to this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use immediately.

---

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Personal Information You Provide

When you register, make a purchase, or use our services, we collect:

- **Account Information**: Name, email address, phone number, password
- **Profile Information**: Date of birth, gender, profile photo (optional)
- **Shipping Address**: Street address, city, state, postal code, country
- **Payment Information**: Credit/debit card details (processed securely via Stripe)
- **Communication Data**: Messages sent through customer support, reviews, ratings

### 2.2 Automatically Collected Information

When you use the Platform, we automatically collect:

- **Device Information**: IP address, browser type, operating system, device identifiers
- **Usage Data**: Pages visited, products viewed, search queries, clickstream data
- **Location Data**: Approximate location based on IP address; precise location if you enable GPS (optional)
- **Cookies and Tracking**: Session cookies, analytics cookies, preference cookies

### 2.3 Information from Third Parties

- **Social Media Login**: If you sign in via Google or Apple, we receive your name, email, and profile picture
- **Payment Processors**: Stripe provides us with transaction confirmation data (no full card numbers stored)
- **Analytics Providers**: Google Analytics, Mixpanel (anonymized usage statistics)

We use your information for the following purposes:

### 3.1 Service Provision
- Create and manage your account

### 3.2 Personalization
- Display products in your preferred language

### 3.4 Analytics and Improvement
- Analyze user behavior to improve the Platform
- Monitor platform performance and fix errors
- Conduct research and development
- Generate anonymized reports for merchants

### 3.5 Security and Fraud Prevention
- Detect and prevent fraudulent transactions
- Verify user identity
- Enforce our Terms and Conditions
- Comply with legal obligations

## 4. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

For users in the European Union (EU) or European Economic Area (EEA), we process your data based on:

1. **Contract Performance**: To fulfill orders and provide services
2. **Legitimate Interests**: To improve the Platform, prevent fraud, and ensure security
3. **Consent**: For marketing emails, cookies, and optional features (you can withdraw consent anytime)
4. **Legal Obligation**: To comply with tax, accounting, and legal requirements

## 5. How We Share Your Information

We **do not sell** your personal information. We share data only in the following circumstances:

### 5.1 Service Providers
- **Payment Processors**: Stripe (for payment processing)
- **Shipping Partners**: DHL, FedEx, local couriers (for order fulfillment)
- **Cloud Hosting**: AWS, Google Cloud (for data storage)
- **Email Services**: SendGrid, Mailgun (for transactional emails)
- **Analytics**: Google Analytics, Mixpanel (anonymized data)

All service providers are contractually obligated to protect your data.

### 5.2 Merchants/Store Owners
- **Order Information**: Merchants see your name, shipping address, and order details to fulfill purchases
- **Aggregated Data**: Merchants may see anonymized sales statistics

### 5.3 Legal Requirements
We may disclose your information if required by law:
- To comply with court orders, subpoenas, or legal processes
- To protect our rights, safety, or property
- To investigate fraud or security incidents

### 5.4 Business Transfers
If ComSpace is acquired, merged, or sold, your data may be transferred to the new entity (you will be notified).

## 7. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as necessary to provide services and comply with legal obligations:

- **Account Data**: Until you delete your account (or 3 years of inactivity)
- **Order History**: 7 years (for tax and accounting purposes)
- **Marketing Emails**: Until you unsubscribe
- **Cookies**: As specified in our Cookie Policy (typically 1-2 years)

After retention periods, data is anonymized or securely deleted.

## 10. Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security practices:

- **Encryption**: HTTPS (TLS 1.3) for data in transit; AES-256 for data at rest
- **Authentication**: Hashed passwords (bcrypt), JWT tokens, OAuth 2.0
- **Access Controls**: Role-based permissions, least privilege principle
- **Monitoring**: Real-time intrusion detection, security audits
- **Compliance**: PCI-DSS (via Stripe), GDPR, CCPA

However, no system is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.
