## 3. Free Trial

### 3.1 Trial Period
- **Duration**: 14 days (free, no credit card required)
- **Access**: Full access to **Professional Plan** features
- **Limitations**: Maximum 20 products during trial

### 3.2 After Trial Ends
- You must choose a paid plan to continue
- If no plan is selected, your store will be **paused** (not deleted)

## 4. Billing and Payment

### 4.1 Billing Cycle
- **Monthly Plans**: Billed on the same day each month
- **Annual Plans**: Billed once per year (discounted rate)
- **First Charge**: Immediately upon subscription (no prorated refunds)

### 4.2 Payment Methods
- Credit/Debit Card (Visa, Mastercard, Amex)
- Bank Transfer (for annual plans only, contact sales@comspace.com)
- PayPal (coming soon)

### 4.3 Automatic Renewal
- Subscriptions **auto-renew** unless canceled
- You will be charged automatically on the renewal date
- Email reminder sent **7 days before renewal**
- **Grace Period**: 3 days to update payment if charge fails

### 4.4 Failed Payments
If your payment fails:
1. **Day 1**: Email notification sent, retry payment
2. **Day 3**: Second email, retry payment
3. **Day 7**: Your store is **paused** (customers cannot access)
4. **Day 14**: Store is **suspended** (may be deleted after 30 days)

Update your payment method at: **Dashboard ‚Üí Settings ‚Üí Billing**

## 7. Upgrades and Downgrades

### 7.1 Upgrading Your Plan
- **Immediate Effect**: New features available instantly
- **Prorated Charge**: You pay the difference for the remaining billing period
- **Example**: Upgrade from Starter ($29) to Professional ($79) on Day 15 of the month ‚Üí You pay ~$25 for the remaining 15 days

### 7.2 Downgrading Your Plan
- **Effective Next Billing Cycle**: Downgrade takes effect on your next renewal date
- **No Refunds**: You keep current plan features until the end of the billing period
- **Feature Loss Warning**: You'll be notified of features you'll lose
- **Data Limits**: If you exceed new plan limits (e.g., 100 products on Starter), you must delete excess items

## 8. Cancellation and Refunds

### 8.1 How to Cancel
1. Log in to your merchant dashboard
2. Go to **Settings ‚Üí Billing ‚Üí Subscription**
3. Click **"Cancel Subscription"**
4. Confirm cancellation
5. You'll receive a confirmation email

### 8.2 What Happens After Cancellation
- Your store remains **active until the end of the current billing period**
- After that, your store is **paused** (not deleted)
- Customers cannot access your store
- You can reactivate by subscribing again within **90 days**
- After 90 days, your store and data may be **permanently deleted**

### 8.3 Refund Policy
- **No Refunds**: Subscription fees are **non-refundable**
- **Exceptions**: Refunds may be issued at our discretion for:
  - Billing errors
  - Technical issues preventing use
  - Duplicate charges
- **Request a Refund**: Email billing@comspace.com within **7 days** of the charge

### 8.4 Annual Plan Cancellation
- Annual plans are **non-refundable**
- If you cancel mid-year, you forfeit the remaining months
- **No prorated refunds** for unused time
